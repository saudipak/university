About-Us

Bagmati State University (BSU) is a leading institution of higher education located in the heart of Bagmati State, Nepal. Established with the vision of fostering academic excellence and nurturing future leaders, BSU is committed to providing quality education, conducting groundbreaking research, and serving the community. Our university offers a diverse range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including arts, sciences, engineering, business, education, and more. With a dedicated faculty comprising experts and scholars from around the world, we strive to create a dynamic learning environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation. At BSU, we believe in the transformative power of education to shape lives and build a better society. Our students are not only equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary for their chosen careers but also instilled with values of integrity, compassion, and social responsibility. As a research-driven institution, BSU is at the forefront of cutting-edge discoveries and innovation.
